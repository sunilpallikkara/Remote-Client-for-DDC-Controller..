The project integrates DDC controllers or Programmable Logical Controllers to Sinric Mobile App and Dashboard over a device called 'Modbus Integrator' for remote access.
The DDC or PLC controllers must be configured as MODBUS Slave to integrate. The Modbus Integrator device is configured as master. 
The 'Modbus Integrator' integrates your DDC or PLC to Alexa, Google Home and SmartThings.
